Omelette

Description:

A sweet omelette with berries is a delightful and nutritious breakfast or brunch option. This dish features a fluffy, lightly sweetened omelette filled with a medley of fresh berries, offering a perfect balance of sweetness and tartness. It's a quick and easy recipe that's sure to brighten your morning.

Ingredients:

  • For the omelette:
    • 3 large eggs
    • 1 tbsp sugar
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 2 tbsp milk or cream
    • pinch of salt
    • 1 tbsp butter
  • For the cream filling:
    • 1/2 cup heavy cream
    • 1 tbsp powdered sugar
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • For the berry filling:
    • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, blackberries)
    • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • For garnish:
    • Powdered sugar for dusting (optional)
    • Fresh mint leaves (optional)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the cream filling:
    • In a medium bowl, whip the heavy cream with the powdered sugar and vanilla extract until soft peaks form. Set aside.
  2. Prepare the berry filling:
    • Wash and dry the berries. If using strawberries, hull and slice them.
    • If desired, drizzle the berries with honey or maple syrup and set aside.
  3. Make the omelette mixture:
    • In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, milk or cream, and a pinch of salt until well combined and slightly frothy.
  4. Cook the omelette:
    • Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the butter, swirling to coat the pan evenly.
    • Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and cook without stirring for about 2-3 minutes, or until the edges start to set.
    • Gently lift the edges of the omelette with a spatula, allowing the uncooked egg to flow underneath. Continue to cook until the omelette is just set but still slightly soft in the center, about another 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add the fillings and fold:
    • Spoon the whipped cream and mixed berries onto one half of the omelette.
    • Carefully fold the other half of the omelette over the fillings using a spatula.
  6. Finish and serve:
    • Slide the omelette onto a plate.
    • Dust with powdered sugar and garnish with fresh mint leaves, if desired.
    • Serve immediately and enjoy!

This sweet omelette with berries and cream is a luxurious and delicious way to start your day. Enjoy!
